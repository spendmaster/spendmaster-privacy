Data Collection Overview

SpendMaster is designed with a local-first, privacy-focused architecture.

  • We do not operate backend servers that collect your budgeting or financial records.
  • We do not require you to create an account to use core features.
  • Your expenses, income, budgets, categories, and other finance records stay on your device.

Collecting and Using Your Personal Data

Types of Data Collected

Personal Data

SpendMaster does not collect personal data such as your name, email, phone number, or your budgeting and financial records as part of the core app functionality. Any finance information you enter into the app is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to us.

Usage Data

We do not directly collect usage data ourselves. However, third-party SDKs used for advertising (and in some cases, measurement/analytics) may collect limited device and usage information independently, as described below.

AI-Powered Features and Third-Party AI Providers

SpendMaster offers optional AI-powered features that allow you to interact with a financial assistant.

How AI Works

  • AI features are optional and can be enabled/disabled in the app settings.
  • You may configure your own third-party AI provider (for example, by entering an API key).
  • If AI is unavailable or disabled, the app may provide responses using on-device intelligence.

Data Handling for AI

  • Only the text you voluntarily submit in the AI chat may be sent to the AI provider you select.
  • SpendMaster does not receive, store, log, or retain your AI conversations on our servers.
  • AI requests are sent from your device to the chosen provider, and that provider processes the data under its own policies.

We encourage you to review the privacy policy and terms of any AI provider you choose to use.

AI Disclaimer

AI-generated responses are provided for informational purposes only and do not constitute financial, investment, tax, or legal advice. You are responsible for decisions you make based on information provided by the app or any AI feature.

Email Communications and Support

If You choose to contact Us via email (for example, to request support, report an issue, recover access, or submit a feature request), We may receive information You voluntarily provide, such as:

  • Your email address
  • Your username
  • The content of your message

This information is received only through email communication and is not collected through the SpendMaster app itself.

We use this information solely to respond to Your request and provide support. We do not store this information in the app, use it for advertising, analytics, or share it with third parties.

For security reasons, We recommend that users do not send sensitive information such as passwords via email.
